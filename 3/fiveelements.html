Cantina Review

I went to the Taco Bell Cantina, here's what it was like.

The Crowd

The crowd was entertaining to watch, two couples on dates and a group of teenagers. 5/5

Interior Design

Lots of exposed brick and wood. Especially appreciated were the the eight outlet boxes along each wall. 5/5

The Music

The music wasn't very good, I wore my headphones instead of listening to it. 2/5

The Food

My five layer burrito was well above average quality for an already delicious dish. The rolled tacos, though, were not great. 4/5
